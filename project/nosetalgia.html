Project Description

Nosetalgia is a group project born from a shared passion for films and music, and the observation that there is limited local space for thoughtful cultural discussion. I originated the idea, wrote the project proposal, and defined the initial content workflow. We decided on a content-first, mobile-first approach to validate the concept quickly, launching on TikTok and Instagram for rapid testing and reach. Currently, we plan to publish reviews and commentary while iterating on a full web/app version with archives and community features.
